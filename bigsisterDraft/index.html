About

It was summer, 2016. A group of college friends, enjoying some drinks, began talking about dating apps. Inevitably, the women convinced the males to unholster their phones and reveal their Tinder profiles. As you can imagine, everyone laughed and the women had some feedback for the men (or should we say boys). After a few minutes of personal questions, bio rewriting, and some engineered photo-ops, the boys' profiles underwent a complete transformation.

That warm summer night in a college town near Detroit... boys became men. And it only took a few minutes.
